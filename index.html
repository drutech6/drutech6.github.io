About The Property

Nestled in a peaceful and friendly neighborhood, our vacation home offers the perfect blend of comfort and convenience. This charming house is ideal for families, couples, or anyone looking to relax and unwind away from the hustle and bustle. It is just 26 minutes from Piarco International Airport, 15 minutes to Downtown Port of Spain, 35 minutes to Maracas Beach, and 3 minutes from the bustling Eastern Main Road and the Croisee, where groceries, bakeries, banks and transport are easily accessed.
