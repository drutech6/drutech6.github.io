About Us

Leonella's Vacation Home is a comfortable two-bedroom rental located in a quiet residential area of San Juan, Trinidad. It is just 26 minutes from Piarco International Airport, 15 minutes to Downtown Port of Spain, 35 minutes to Maracas Beach, and 3 minutes from the bustling Eastern Main Road and the Croisee, where groceries, bakeries, banks and transport are easily accessed.

Location

Leonella's Vacation Home is situated at No. 7 Campo Street Extension, San Juan, Trinidad. The quiet neighborhood ensures restful nights while still being minutes away from the lively Croisee, where you’ll find groceries, bakeries, stores, banks and public transport. Major routes to Piarco International Airport, Downtown Port of Spain, and Maracas Beach are all within easy reach.

House Rules

  • Smoking is NOT ALLOWED inside. Guests may smoke outside.
  • Present your ID, Passport or Temporary Residence Card upon check-in.
  • Inform us the night before departure of your intended check-out time.
  • All waste must be placed in the provided garbage cans outside. Collection is daily early morning, Monday-Friday.
  • Switch off all electrical appliances before leaving the house.
  • Conserve water; supply automatically switches to the water tank during shortages.
  • Guests are responsible for any loss or damage caused by themselves or their companions.
  • Park vehicles only in the designated area.
  • Maximum occupancy is 4 persons. Extra charge of $25 USD per additional person.
  • Pets are not allowed inside or on the grounds.
  • Keep noise to a minimum to respect our neighbors.
  • Maintain a clean and sanitary environment.
  • Secure the gates when entering or leaving the premises.

These rules ensure a safe and pleasant stay. Enjoy your vacation!
